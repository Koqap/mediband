# MEDIBAND: Presentation Guide for Nurses

## What is MEDIBAND?
**MEDIBAND** is a smart, table-top assistant designed to make checking vital signs faster and easier. Think of it as an automated partner that measures a patient's **Heart Rate** and **Oxygen Levels** and instantly records them for you.

Instead of manually counting pulses or writing down numbers, MEDIBAND does the work and sends the data directly to a digital dashboard.

---

## How It Helps You (Clinical Benefits)
*   **Saves Time**: Automates the 15-second counting process, allowing you to focus on the patient.
*   **Reduces Errors**: Eliminates manual counting mistakes and transcription errors.
*   **Instant Records**: Automatically saves the results to a history logâ€”no more paper charting for these vitals.
*   **AI "Second Opinion"**: Uses smart technology to analyze the heart rhythm and suggest if a patient needs closer attention (Normal, Warning, or Critical).

---

## How It Works (The Workflow)

### 1. Start the Check-Up
You have two easy ways to start:
*   **On the Device**: Simply press the **BOOT button** on the Mediband box.
*   **On the Computer**: Click **"Start Check-Up"** on the dashboard screen.

### 2. The Measurement (15 Seconds)
*   The device will light up and show a **"MEDIBAND"** intro.
*   You will see a moving **heart graph** (EKG) on the device screen.
*   The computer screen will show a **progress bar** that matches the device perfectly, so you know exactly how much time is left.

### 3. The Result
*   After 15 seconds, the device beeps (visually) and shows **"FINISHED"**.
*   **On the Device**: You will see the **Average BPM** and the calculated **Risk Level** (Normal, Warning, Critical) right on the screen. This allows you to triage even if the computer is off.
*   **On the Computer**: The dashboard instantly updates with the full analysis and an **AI Health Insight**.
*   You can then **Save** the record or **Print** it immediately.

---

## Simple Explanation of the Tech (If Asked)
*   **The Box (Device)**: This is the sensor unit. It reads the patient's pulse and acts like a mini-computer.
*   **The Connection**: It uses Wi-Fi to talk to the main computer, just like your phone connects to the internet.
*   **The Brain (AI)**: We use a secure AI system to look at the heart patterns, similar to how an expert cardiologist might look for irregularities, giving you a quick "risk score" to help you triage patients.

---

## Standalone Mode (No Computer Needed)
If the Wi-Fi or website is down, **don't worry**. The Mediband device is smart enough to calculate the risk level itself. You can perform the full check-up and get the **Risk Result** directly on the device screen.
